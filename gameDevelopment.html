Riddle Game

This is a 3D first-person PC game where the player progresses through a series of rooms, where they must find a particular object. There is a riddle in each room describing the object that must be found. To find the object, the player must solve the riddle. Once they find the object, they advance to the next level.

Energy Conservation Simulation

This is a 3D first-person walkthrough experience aimed to raise awareness on energy consumption and conservation. In this simulation, the player walks from room to room and see what the best uses are for the appliances in each room.

Speed Dodger

This is a mobile game where the player in the red car starts off with a score of 0 and must dodge the oncoming cars to increase their score. If they hit one of the cars, the game is over.
