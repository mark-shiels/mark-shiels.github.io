Bike Hire App Interface

I designed an app interface aimed to reduce the carbon footprint of college students. The app is a bike hiring service that encourages students to cycle to reduce the carbon footprint. It will show the user nearby hire and return stations, suggested routes, as well as account information such as credit remaining, activity and discounts available.

Software Used
Adobe Illustrator
